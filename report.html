Recommendations:

  1. Appliance Usage Optimization:
    It is advised to assess the necessity of operating ceiling fans for extended periods.   2. Lighting Efficiency:
    While utilizing energy-efficient LED bulbs is a positive step, we recommend consistently turning off lights when leaving a room. Installing motion sensor switches in high-traffic areas will contribute to energy savings.
  3. Heating and Cooling:
    Efficient management of your electric heater is essential. Utilize a programmable thermostat to regulate temperature settings during different times of the day, ensuring energy is not expended unnecessarily.
